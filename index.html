About Us

A Pizza Paradise is one of the most recognisable icons of Africa. Whichever way you slice it, Pizza Paradise is an industry leader in the quick service pizza game. The first Pizza Paradise outlet opened its doors in 1998 in Nairobi, Kenya, and rose quickly in popularity. Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients, Pizza Paradise has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily one of the first choices in quick service pizza in Africa.
